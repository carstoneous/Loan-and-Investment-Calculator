Apartment Loan and Investment Simulator

This tool allows you to simulate the financial aspects of your apartment valued at €600,000 with an existing debt of €300,000. Input your monthly rent income (default €2,500, adjustable), adjust loan duration, interest rates, amortization types, and simulate property value growth, rent income growth, and other variables—all in euros—to see their impact on your finances. It also calculates total accumulated cash flow, the value of investing it, and your total asset value.
